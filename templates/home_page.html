Welome to the Mobile Salon!

You don't go to the Salon...Instead the Salon comes to you...


The purpose of this website is for people to get their haircuts done without having to go to the salon and risking catching COVID-19. The idea is that salon owners transform their business into a mobile truck that can satisfy a customer’s needs by going to their house and reducing the chances of infection. Below are the functions of this website. By simplifying the process of getting a haircut, one’s life becomes much easier during social distancing. For viewing the website you must have a login and you can do that from the register page. All accounts will be taken down after the hackathon. These are the following features: 1) To view all the shop listings you can go to Browse Shops. 2) To create a shop you can go to create shop. 3) If you own a shop you will be able to delete it from the listings page and view bookings in the bookings page. If you are trying out this site it is preffered that you make your own shop and make some booking so you can see how the interface looks for the business owner.
